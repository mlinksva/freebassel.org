Bassel Khartabil was reportedly sentenced to death in Syria

Khartabil, a software developer and active Creative Commons supporter, has reportedly been sentenced to death by Syria's Assad Regime. He was imprisoned in 2012 as he was working on developing a digital rendering of Palmyra, an ancient city bombed and destroyed by ISIS. Since his imprisonment, the MIT Media Lab has been continuing his work, and through the Huffington Post, Director Joichi Ito wrote of his intentions to hire Khartabil back in October, voicing his hope that Khartabil is still alive and asking for his immediate release.
