Developers

The FREEBASSEL campaign is looking for a part-time volunteer web developer to help update and maintain the website at freebassel.org.

Requirements
  • Experience with: Ruby, JavaScript, git, HTML, CSS, SCSS, Markdown, Jekyll, Foundation, and Inkscape.
  • Excellent English writing and reading comprehension. (Knowledge of French or Arabic is a plus.)
  • A Google account and a Github account. (A Twitter account is a plus).
Responsibilities
  • Commit a minimum of 1 hour per week to development of Freebassel.org.
  • Monitor news about Bassel Khartabil and add relevant web articles to the site.
  • Keep the supporters list up to date.
  • Occasionally respond to website changes or feature requests via email or via Github issues.
